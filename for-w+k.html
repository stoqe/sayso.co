FOBO & THE GOATEST
a multi-concept creative campaign for old spice

i'm supes salty i missed the eyeka old spice creative comp. reading the brief cre·aved me, so i created anyway. old spice want a manly hyperbolic metaphoric campaign for their greatest product ever, so here's mine.

about FOBO & THE GOATEST

note: this work is 100% original and 100% mine. also: i'm presenting my words & concepts, not my [average] graphics skills. my [average] graphics skills simply add context to my words & concepts.

the 3 key concepts in my creatives are...

  1. fobo (fear of body odour): fobo is the problem old spice solves presented as a funny, original acronym which also highlights the serious, familar, and relateable matter for men: fear of body odour. "fobo" takes the modern "fomo" format (universally-known "fear of missing out") and adds the historic "body odour" acronym "b.o" (making this an acronym inside an acronym; does that make it a macronym? did i just make that word up too? should "macronym" be an old spice ad of it's own?). "fobo" gives an identity to the problem old spice solves in an easy-to-remember, instantly-relatable way. fobo is the powerful, invisible character: fear.

  2. the goat: old spice already has great characters. so what beats great? the greatest. and what represent "the greatest" in today's ways? a goat; literally. which of course metaphorically means "the greatest of all time". so: the old spice goat (character) can represent old spice at the brand level and/or simultaneously at the product level. let's pause here for some reflection... ... ... this campain present the-greatest-brand-of-all-time as a goat presenting the-greatist-deoderant-of-all-time while reinforcing fear, reminding of the probem, providing the solution, owning original concepts (fobo), and self-awarding titles (the goatest). that's goat!

    furthermore, my themed-goat creatives (the first 7; costumed) symbolise famous g.o.a.t's — muhammad ali, dirty harry, ghost busters, apollo 8, karate kid, star wars, babe ruth — to further-align and reinforce old spice as a greatest. these themed creatives can be extended endlessly through other famous g.o.a.ts. for-and-from all demographics. each themed-goat quote is a twist on the most famous quote from these famous g.o.a.ts. the standard goat creatives (final 3) are 100% original copy.

  3. the goatest: i'm still working on [at least] 1 other creative piece for this collection with a slightly different angle — "the goat just got goated. meet the goatest" — where the current old spice character (the old spice man; the current g.o.a.t) gets "goated" by the new goat character. this represents old spice as the current g.o.a.t of deoderant who rise to even greater heights with their new product... which is even goater. it's the goatest. and it doesn't get any goater than that...
