FOBO & THE GOATEST
a multi-concept creative campaign for old spice

i'm supes salty i missed the eyeka old spice creative comp. reading the brief cre·aved me, so i created anyway. old spice want a manly hyperbolic metaphoric campaign for their greatest product ever, so here's mine.

about FOBO & THE GOATEST

note: this work is 100% original and 100% mine. i'm presenting my words & concepts, not my graphics skills. my average graphics skills simply add context to my words & concepts.

the 3 key concepts in my creatives are...

  1. fobo (Fear Of Body Odour): fobo is a brand new industry acronym (as far as i can tell) and the problem old spice solves. "fobo" (the acronym) plays off the universally-known "fomo" while also incorporating the universally-known "body odour" acronym "b.o" (an acronym inside an acronym; does that make it a macronym? and did i just make another word up?). "fobo" gives an identity to the problem, is easy-to-remember, is instantly relatable, and is a powerful, invisible character: fear.

  2. the goat: old spice already has great characters. there's only one thing better than great — the greatest — and the goat (visual character) represents old spice's new product as the Greatest Of All Time. also: the goat character can also represent old spice the brand as the Greatest Of All Time deoderant brand. it's the g.o.a.t presenting the g.o.a.t! and my visuals use other iconic g.o.a.t's to make that statement and confirm old spice as the greatest deoderant (and brand). my 7 themed goat creatives symbolise famous g.o.a.t's — muhammad ali, dirty harry, ghost busters, apollo 8, karate kid, star wars, babe ruth — and play on words from their most famous quotes. (these themed creatives could be extended endlessly with other famously-known g.o.a.ts. targeting different generations too.) my 3 standard goat creatives ditch the themes and contain 100% original copy.

  3. the goatest: i'm still working on [at least] 1 other creative piece for this collection with a slightly different angle — "the goat just got goated. meet the goatest" — where the current old spice characters (who currently are the greatest of all time) get goated by the new old spice character (the physical goat). this represent the current new spice range being the current greatest of all time but the new old spice product as even greater. even goater. it's the goatest. and it doesn't get any goater than that...
